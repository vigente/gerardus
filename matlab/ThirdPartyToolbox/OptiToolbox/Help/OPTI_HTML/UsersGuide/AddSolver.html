Adding Your Solver to OPTI

Open Source / Free Academic Solvers

If you want an OPTI Interface written to your solver I would be happy to collaborate! Solvers I can work with include, but are not limited to:

- C / C++ Solvers
- FORTRAN Solvers
- MATLAB Solvers
- .NET (C# / VB.NET)
- Standalone Executables

If the solver requires a MEX Interface I am also available to work with you in developing this. The only requirement is that your solver must be:

- Open Source or
- Available under a free Academic License (with a license period of not less than 90 days, preferably 1 year).

Note I prefer not to work with solvers which are problem size limited as these aren't really 'free'. Even if your code is old, originally compiled in Linux, and/or in need of an upgrade I can help! Your solver just has to fill a niche within the OPTI framework.

If you would like more information, feel free to contact me to discuss your requirements. If your package is open-source, all services are free!

Commercial Solvers

I have written MATLAB and MEX interfaces for a number of optimization solvers, both for the open-source community as well as commercial solvers. If you are interested in a MATLAB interface to your solver I can develop this for you, provide end-user documentation and support. Send me an email to discuss your requirements.
