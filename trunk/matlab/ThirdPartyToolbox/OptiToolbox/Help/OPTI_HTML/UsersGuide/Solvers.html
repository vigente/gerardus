Optimization Solvers

The core function of OPTI Toolbox is to provide a high level interface to the large number of solvers available for optimization. While the focus of the toolbox is on the open-source community solvers, commercial solvers have been interfaced as well where academic licenses are available.

For more information on the solvers supplied with and interfaced to OPTI, consult the following OPTI Wiki page:
